NABEEL AGENCY — COMPLETE DASHBOARD USER GUIDE
==============================================
This guide explains how registered users and team members can use their dashboards
to add, view, update, and delete data. Use this as a step-by-step reference.


================================================================
SECTION 1: USER DASHBOARD GUIDE
================================================================

The User Dashboard is for all registered and verified clients of Nabeel Agency.
After logging in at /login, users with the "user" role are redirected to /user-dashboard.
Sidebar sections: Overview, My Projects, Applied Jobs, AI Assistant, Profile, Support.


----------------------------------------------------------------
1.1 OVERVIEW PAGE (/user-dashboard)
----------------------------------------------------------------
Read-only stats dashboard. Shows:
- Total projects submitted
- Projects by status: Pending, In Review, Approved, Rejected, Completed
- Total revenue (sum of costs across projects)
- Average progress percentage
- 3 most recent projects with status badges

Nothing can be added or deleted here. Navigate to My Projects for full management.


----------------------------------------------------------------
1.2 MY PROJECTS PAGE (/user-dashboard/projects)
----------------------------------------------------------------

HOW TO SUBMIT A NEW PROJECT REQUEST:
1. Click the "New Project" button (top-right corner).
2. Fill in the dialog form:
   - Project Name (required)
   - Project Type (required): Web Development, Mobile App, UI/UX Design,
     Digital Marketing, SEO Optimization, E-Commerce, Branding, Content Creation,
     Cloud Solutions, Cybersecurity, AI Integration, Data Analytics, Custom Solution
   - Budget Range (required): choose the bracket that fits your project
   - Deadline (required): pick a target completion date
   - Project Details (required): describe goals, features, references, requirements
   - Attachments (optional): upload any files (designs, docs, PDFs, images, etc.)
3. Click "Submit Request".
4. Project appears with status "Pending" while admin reviews it.

HOW TO VIEW YOUR PROJECTS:
- All submitted projects appear as cards on this page.
- Filter by status: All, Pending, In Review, Approved, Rejected, Completed.
- Each card shows: name, type, status badge, progress bar, deadline, budget, payment status.
- Click the eye icon (View Details) to open the full detail modal:
  description, attached files with download links, assigned team members,
  progress %, total cost, amount paid, payment status.

HOW TO DELETE A PROJECT:
- You can only delete projects in "Pending" or "Rejected" status.
- Click the trash icon on the project card and confirm.
- Approved / In Review / Completed projects cannot be deleted by users.
  Contact support if needed.

PROJECT STATUS MEANINGS:
- Pending     -- submitted, waiting for admin review
- In Review   -- admin is currently evaluating the request
- Approved    -- agency accepted, work has begun
- Rejected    -- not accepted; you can submit a revised request
- Completed   -- project finished and delivered


----------------------------------------------------------------
1.3 APPLIED JOBS PAGE (/user-dashboard/applied-jobs)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Shows all job applications you submitted through the public Careers page.

HOW TO VIEW YOUR JOB APPLICATIONS:
- Each card shows: job title, department, experience level, date applied, status badge.
- Admin notes (if any) appear in a highlighted box on the card.
- Click "Resume" to view the resume you submitted.
- Click "View Job" to see the original job listing.

APPLICATION STATUS MEANINGS:
- Pending     -- submitted, not yet reviewed
- Reviewing   -- admin is reviewing your application
- Shortlisted -- selected for further consideration
- Hired       -- you have been offered a position; account upgraded to "team" automatically
- Rejected    -- not selected for this position

HOW TO APPLY FOR A NEW JOB:
- Go to the public Careers page at /careers.
- Browse open positions and click "Apply Now" on any listing.
- Fill in your details and upload your resume (PDF or DOC).

NOTE: You cannot delete or withdraw a submitted application.
Use the Support page to raise a concern.


----------------------------------------------------------------
1.4 PROFILE PAGE (/user-dashboard/profile)
----------------------------------------------------------------

HOW TO UPDATE YOUR NAME:
1. Click the Edit (pencil) icon next to your name.
2. Type the new name.
3. Click "Save".

HOW TO CHANGE YOUR PROFILE AVATAR:
1. Click on your profile picture or the camera icon overlay.
2. Select an image file (JPG, PNG, or WebP recommended).
3. The image uploads automatically and appears across the entire dashboard.

HOW TO CHANGE YOUR PASSWORD:
1. Scroll to the "Change Password" section.
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter and confirm your new password (minimum 8 characters).
4. Click "Update Password".

Forgot your password? Click "Forgot Password" on /login.
A reset email will be sent to you.

NOTIFICATION PREFERENCES:
Toggles on the Profile page let you enable or disable email notifications
and project update alerts. These are saved locally.


----------------------------------------------------------------
1.5 SUPPORT PAGE (/user-dashboard/support)
----------------------------------------------------------------

HOW TO CREATE A SUPPORT TICKET:
1. Click "New Ticket".
2. Fill in:
   - Subject: a short title for your issue
   - Category: Technical / Billing / Account / Project / General
   - Priority: Low / Normal / High / Urgent
   - Message: describe your issue in detail
3. Click "Submit Ticket". It appears in the list with status "Open".

HOW TO VIEW AND REPLY TO TICKETS:
- Click a ticket card to expand the full conversation thread.
- Type your reply at the bottom and click "Send".
- Team responses appear in the same thread.

TICKET STATUS MEANINGS:
- Open        -- submitted, awaiting response
- In Progress -- a team member is working on it
- Resolved    -- issue has been addressed
- Closed      -- ticket closed

The FAQ accordion on this page answers many common questions about
projects, billing, and accounts. Check it before submitting a ticket.


----------------------------------------------------------------
1.6 AI ASSISTANT PAGE (/user-dashboard/ai-assistant)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Full-page chat with Nova AI. The AI has real-time access to YOUR data:
- Projects: name, status, progress, deadline, cost, payment
- Applied jobs: role applied for, status, admin notes
- Profile: name, email, role
- Agency knowledge base

WHAT NOVA AI CAN HELP YOU WITH:
- "What is the status of my website project?"
- "What is my outstanding balance?"
- "Which of my job applications is shortlisted?"
- "How do I submit a new project request?"
- Step-by-step guidance for any dashboard action
- Navigate you to specific pages

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to "AI Assistant" in the sidebar.
2. Type your question and press Enter (or click the send button).
   Shift+Enter adds a new line without sending.
3. Use quick prompt buttons on the welcome screen for one-click queries.
4. The info sidebar (right) shows suggested prompts and accessible data.
5. Collapse the sidebar with the arrow button in the chat header.
6. Click the trash icon to clear the conversation and start fresh.

TIPS:
- Be specific: "Show my pending projects" beats "show projects"
- Ask comparisons: "Which project has the highest progress?"
- Chat history is saved and restored on return.
  A "Previous conversation" divider marks past messages.


================================================================
SECTION 2: TEAM DASHBOARD GUIDE
================================================================

The Team Dashboard is for employees and contractors hired by Nabeel Agency.
After logging in, "team" role users are redirected to /team.
Sidebar: Overview, Projects, Tasks, Clients, Resources, Calendar,
         Reports, Support, AI Assistant, Applied Jobs, Settings.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.1 OVERVIEW PAGE (/team)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Read-only summary of work assigned to you:
- Number of assigned client projects
- Active tasks
- Quick-access links to most-used sections
Updates in real time -- new project or task assignments appear without refreshing.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.2 PROJECTS PAGE (/team/projects)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Has two tabs: "Client Requests" and "Portfolio Projects".

CLIENT REQUESTS TAB:
- Shows all client project requests assigned to you by the admin.
- Each card: project name, client name, status, progress bar, deadline, budget.
- Click "View Details" for the full detail page:
  description, downloadable attachments, assigned team list,
  client contact info, payment status, progress.
- Team members cannot change project status, cost, or progress (admin-only).

PORTFOLIO PROJECTS TAB:
- Shows the agency published portfolio and work samples.
- Each card: title, category, description, visibility (public/private).
- Detail page: images, live URL, GitHub link, tech stack, timeline.
- Team members cannot create, edit, or delete portfolio projects (admin-only).

If an expected project is not showing, contact admin via Support.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.3 TASKS PAGE -- KANBAN BOARD (/team/tasks)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Four columns: To Do | In Progress | In Review | Completed

HOW TO ADD A NEW TASK:
1. Click "+ Add Task" at the top of any column (or the floating "+" button).
2. Fill in:
   - Title (required)
   - Description (optional)
   - Priority: Low / Medium / High / Critical
   - Due Date (optional)
   - Tags (optional) -- e.g. "frontend", "bug", "design"
3. Click "Create Task". It appears in the selected column.

HOW TO MOVE A TASK BETWEEN COLUMNS:
- Drag and drop the task card from one column to another.
- Or open the task card and change the column using the status dropdown.
- Moving a task to "Completed" marks it as done.

HOW TO EDIT A TASK:
1. Click the task card to open the edit dialog.
2. Modify any field (title, description, priority, due date, tags).
3. Click "Save" or "Update".

HOW TO DELETE A TASK:
- Single: click the trash icon on the task card or inside the task dialog, then confirm.
- Bulk: check multiple cards using the checkboxes (visible on hover or in select mode),
  then click "Delete Selected" and confirm.

HOW TO FILTER TASKS:
- Use the search bar to filter by title or tag keyword.
- Use the priority filter dropdown to show only a specific priority level.
- Tasks are colour-coded by priority for quick visual scanning.

COLUMN MEANINGS:
- To Do      -- not started
- In Progress -- currently being worked on
- In Review   -- done, awaiting review or approval
- Completed   -- finished


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.4 RESOURCES PAGE (/team/resources)
----------------------------------------------------------------

A shared file library for uploading, viewing, downloading, and deleting files.

HOW TO UPLOAD A FILE:
1. Click "Upload File" or the "+" button.
2. Select your file (images, PDFs, docs, spreadsheets, videos, ZIPs supported).
3. The file uploads to secure cloud storage automatically.
4. It appears in the library with name, type, size, and upload date.

HOW TO VIEW OR DOWNLOAD A FILE:
- Click the file card or the download icon.
- Images and PDFs may open in a new browser tab for preview.

HOW TO DELETE A FILE:
- Single: click the trash icon on the file card and confirm.
- Bulk: enable select mode, check multiple files, click "Delete Selected" and confirm.
- IMPORTANT: deleted files are permanently removed from cloud storage.
  This cannot be undone.

HOW TO FIND A FILE:
- Use the search bar at the top of the Resources page.
  Type the file name or part of it to filter the list.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.5 APPLIED JOBS PAGE (/team/applied-jobs)
----------------------------------------------------------------

View your original job application history as a team member.

- Each card: job title, department, experience level, date applied, status.
- Admin notes appear if left by the admin.
- Click "Resume" to view the resume you submitted.
- Click "View Job" to see the original job listing.
- "Hired" status means this application led to your current team role.

To apply for additional positions, visit /careers from the public site.


----------------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------------

Full-page chat with Nova AI (emerald theme for team). The team AI has access to:
- Your assigned client projects (full details, timelines, client info, attachments)
- Agency portfolio projects
- Your team profile (name, position, department, skills)
- Agency knowledge base

WHAT NOVA AI CAN HELP TEAM MEMBERS WITH:
- "What client projects am I currently assigned to?"
- "Which project has the earliest deadline?"
- "Tell me about the e-commerce project for Client X"
- "How do I add a task to the Kanban board?"
- "How do I bulk delete files in Resources?"
- "How do I move a task to In Review?"
- Step-by-step guidance for any team dashboard operation

HOW TO USE:
1. Go to "AI Assistant" in the sidebar.
2. Type your question and press Enter (Shift+Enter for a new line).
3. Use quick prompt buttons on the welcome screen for one-click queries.
4. Collapse the info sidebar with the arrow button in the header.
5. Click the trash icon to start a fresh conversation.

Chat history is saved and restored when you return.
Data is always live and up to date.


----------------------------------------------------------------
2.7 SUPPORT PAGE (/team/support)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Internal help resources for team members:
- IT, HR, and Admin contact cards with email/phone links
- Internal ticket system
- FAQ accordion for common internal questions

HOW TO CREATE AN INTERNAL SUPPORT TICKET:
1. Click "New Ticket".
2. Fill in: Subject, Category (IT / HR / Admin / Other), Priority, Message.

----------------------------------------------------------------

----------------------------------------------------------------

CALENDAR (/team/calendar):
Displays project deadlines, task due dates, and team events. Read-only view.

REPORTS (/team/reports):
Productivity and project summaries -- completed tasks, project timelines, team metrics.

- Update display name

SECTION 3: COMMON QUESTIONS AND ANSWERS
================================================================

Q: How do I log in to my dashboard?
A: Go to /login, enter your email and password, and click "Sign In".
   You are redirected based on your role:
   user -> /user-dashboard, team -> /team, admin -> /admin.

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email.
   A password reset link will arrive by email. Click it to set a new password.

Q: How do I verify my email after registering?
A: After registering you receive a 6-digit OTP code by email.
   Enter the code on the verification page.
   If not received, check spam or click "Resend Code".

Q: How do I submit a project request?
A: Log in, go to My Projects, click "New Project", fill in all required fields
   (name, type, budget, deadline, details), attach files if needed,
   then click "Submit Request".

Q: How do I check my project status?
A: Go to My Projects -- each card shows the current status badge.
   You can also ask Nova AI: "What is the status of my [project name]?"

Q: Can I edit a submitted project request?
A: No. Once submitted, project requests cannot be edited.
   Contact support if changes are needed and the admin can make adjustments.

Q: Why can I not delete my project?
A: Only Pending or Rejected projects can be deleted by users.
   In Review, Approved, and Completed projects are locked.
   Contact support if you need assistance.

Q: How do I upload files with my project?
A: Use the file attachment field inside the "New Project" dialog.
   Multiple files are supported (PDFs, images, Word docs, ZIPs, etc.).

Q: How do I check my billing or payment status?
A: Open any project from My Projects and click "View Details".
   It shows Total Cost, Amount Paid, and Payment Status.
   You can also ask Nova AI for a billing summary.

Q: How do I change my profile picture?
A: Go to the Profile page, click your avatar or the camera icon,
   select a new image, and it uploads and updates automatically.

Q: How do I change my email address?
A: Email changes are not self-service at this time.
   Please contact admin support via the Support page to request an email change.

Q: I am a team member. How do I see my assigned client projects?
A: Go to Projects in your team dashboard.
   The "Client Requests" tab shows all projects the admin has assigned to you.

Q: How do I add a task on the Kanban board?
A: Go to Tasks in the team dashboard.
   Click "+ Add Task" at the top of any column.
   Fill in title, description, priority, and due date, then click "Create Task".

Q: How do I move a task to Completed?
A: Drag the task card and drop it into the "Completed" column.
   Or open the task card and change the column using the status dropdown.

Q: How do I delete multiple tasks at once?
A: Enable select mode (checkbox icon), check the tasks you want,
   then click "Delete Selected" and confirm.

Q: How do I upload a shared file for my team?
A: Go to Resources. Click "Upload File", select the file,
   and it uploads immediately to cloud storage for all team members.

Q: How do I find a file in Resources?
A: Use the search bar at the top of the Resources page and type the file name.

Q: How do I contact support?
A: Users: go to Support in your dashboard and create a new ticket.
   Team members: go to Support in the team dashboard for the internal
   ticket system and IT/HR/Admin contact cards.

Q: Can I use the AI assistant without logging in?
A: Yes. The Nova AI chatbot widget is available on every public page of the website.
   It can answer questions about services, pricing, team, and contact info.
   However, it does not have access to your personal data unless you are
   logged in and using the dashboard AI assistant.

Q: What data can the AI access in the User Dashboard?
A: Your projects (name, status, progress, deadline, cost, payment),
   your job applications (role, status, admin notes),
   your profile (name, email, role), and the agency knowledge base.

Q: What data can the AI access in the Team Dashboard?
A: Your assigned client projects (full details), agency portfolio projects,
   your team profile (name, position, department, skills),
   and the agency knowledge base.

Q: How do I apply for a job at Nabeel Agency?
A: Visit /careers. Browse open positions and click a job to see details.
   Click "Apply Now", fill in your info, upload your resume, and submit.
   A confirmation email is sent to you.

Q: How do I track my job application status?
A: Log in to your dashboard and go to "Applied Jobs".
   Each application card shows the current status.
   The page updates in real time when the admin changes your status.

Q: What happens when my application status becomes "Hired"?
A: Your account is automatically upgraded from "user" to "team".
   On your next login you will be redirected to the Team Dashboard.

Q: How do I search within the dashboard?
A: Press Ctrl+K (Cmd+K on Mac) in any dashboard to open the command palette.
   Type any keyword to find pages, sections, or actions and navigate instantly.

Q: Can I reorder the sidebar links?
A: Yes. Hover over any sidebar link to see the drag handle (grid icon on the left).
   Drag links up or down to reorder them. The order is saved in your browser.

Q: How do I pin a sidebar link to the top?
A: Hover over any sidebar link and click the star icon.
   Pinned links move to a "Pinned" section at the very top of the sidebar
   and persist across sessions.
